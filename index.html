ABOUT SPOONMUNCH

THE MUNCH PLAN


SPOONMUNCH is a little cafe in the middle of Santo Domingo's Guarocuya street with the particular characteristic of having edible spoons. This is what we decided to call the MUNCH PLAN.

You see, cafes often remind us not only of that quiet, nice, comfy place you could almost call Granma’s home, but also the sounds, taste, and of course, our little spoons. The spoons they give us to stir our café or latte, or Frappe, or whatever we were looking for in that café, so that we could know if it is on point or not. But the thing I remember the most about cafes’ spoons is the feeling I get after tasting my café one last time in those spoons, I almost want to bite them. And then I thought, “well why not?”, and that’s where SPOONMUNCH was born, in my stomach.
